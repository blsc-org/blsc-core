# Funding & Support

The Bulgarian Language & Script Corpus (BLSC) is an open, community-driven initiative focused on building long-term linguistic infrastructure for the Bulgarian language.  
To ensure sustainable development, maintenance, and expansion of the project, BLSC welcomes participation through the following funding channels.

---

## 1. Grant Funding (Primary)

BLSC is structured to be fully compatible with national and international funding programs, including:

### • European Union Programs
- **Horizon Europe** – Open science, AI, NLP, cultural heritage, digital infrastructure  
- **Digital Europe Programme (DEP)** – Language resources, data spaces, AI tools  
- **CEF / CEF Telecom** – eTranslation, public-sector language tools  
- **Creative Europe** – Digital culture & preservation of literary heritage  

### • National Funding
- Bulgarian National Science Fund (НСФ)  
- Ministry of Culture – Digital heritage & archives  
- University-driven research programs  
- Public innovation funds  

### • Institutional Partnerships
- Academic consortia  
- Research institutes  
- Cultural, archival, and library institutions  
- Public sector digitization programs  

BLSC maintains an open, modular structure designed to integrate into existing research proposals.

---

## 2. Sponsored Development

Organizations, institutions, and companies may support:

- creation of specialized corpus modules  
- digitization of collections  
- annotation and metadata projects  
- development of NLP tools  
- OCR pipelines for Bulgarian text  
- historical text restoration  

Custom agreements can be arranged while preserving the open-source nature of the project.

---

## 3. Donations & Micro-support

While the project does not rely on donations, community support helps expand the corpus and tools.

Ways to contribute:
- sponsoring specific tasks  
- providing compute or storage  
- supporting student contributors  
- enabling annotation efforts  

Donation links (if enabled later):
- GitHub Sponsors

---

## 4. Non-Financial Contributions

BLSC values open participation as highly as financial support.  
Important contributions include:

- adding public-domain or licensed texts  
- cleaning and normalizing datasets  
- OCR corrections  
- metadata enrichment  
- developing scripts, tools, and pipelines  
- documentation and research notes  
- academic collaboration and supervision  

All contributions are credited transparently.

---

## 5. Funding Principles

BLSC follows strict principles to ensure integrity and openness:

- **Open-source first** – all outputs remain freely accessible (MIT license)  
- **Transparency** – all funded work is documented  
- **Neutrality** – no political, commercial, or institutional influence on the dataset  
- **Ethical standards** – respect for copyright, privacy, and academic integrity
